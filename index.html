Academic Prestige

What Is It?

Academic Prestige is a PVP real-time strategy game akin to OGame. You begin as a freshman who is starting their first year at Georgia Fry University. Your goal is to compete with the other students (players) and earn as much academic prestige as you can.

Earn Prestige

Earning academic prestige can be as simple as finishing a short homework assignment to as complex as earning a Bachelor's degree, or if you're ambtitious enough to earn a PhD.

Study

Enroll in classes at Georgia Fry University to learn more about the key univeristy subjects: English, Mathematics, Humanities, Fine Arts and Communications, Science, and Social Sciences. Finish assignments and exams in these classes to earn prestige.

Collaborate

Collaborating with other students decreases the amount of time needed to to study and finish assignments. Form large study groups and combine your skills to accomplish massive feats such as publishing an academic paper. However, you must watch out. Your fellow students can decrease your academic prestige by stealing your homework, embarrassing you in front of the professors at Georgia Fry University, or even causing you to flunk out of any or all of your classes.

Climb the Ranks

Gaining more prestige than your fellow classmates will show them that you are not to be messed with. Your prestige is a reflection of your knowlege and skills at Georgia Fry University. As you grow, it becomes harder for the other students to stop you as they will be less successful in their attempts to sabotage you.
